.. py:function:: moran_i(adata, coord_keys, obs_key, k=1)

   :param adata: Annotated data matrix.
   :type adata: anndata.AnnData
   :param coord_keys: List of keys in adata.obsm containing spatial coordinates.
   :type coord_keys: list
